Guide on How to Detect Fake News

  • Check the Source: Verify if the news comes from a credible website or organization.
  • Cross-Reference Information: Compare the story with other reputable news sources.
  • Look for Author Credentials: Check if the author is a verified journalist or expert.
  • Analyze the URL: Fake news sites may have slight variations in their URLs.
  • Watch for Bias and Emotional Language: Sensational headlines can be red flags.
  • Verify with Fact-Checking Websites: Use trusted sources to confirm claims.
